Students

Provide student availability by CSV upload or manual rows.

  • Expected headers: id,day,start_slot,end_slot
  • Days should use short names like mon, tue, wed.
  • Slots should be integers.

Click blocks to toggle availability (Mon-Fri, 8:00 AM to 8:00 PM in 30-min slots).

Teachers

Provide teacher availability by CSV upload or manual rows.

  • Expected headers: day,start_slot,end_slot
  • Days should use short names like mon, tue, wed.
  • Slots should be integers.

Click blocks to toggle teacher availability (Mon-Fri, 8:00 AM to 8:00 PM in 30-min slots).
